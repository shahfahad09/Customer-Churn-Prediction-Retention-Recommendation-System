# End-to-End Customer Churn Prediction & Retention Recommendation System

An end-to-end Machine Learning project that predicts whether a telecom customer is likely to leave the company (Customer Churn) based on customer demographics, subscribed services, billing information, contract type, and payment behavior.

The project also provides customer retention recommendations to help businesses reduce churn and improve customer retention.

---

## Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges for telecom companies. Losing existing customers leads to significant revenue loss and acquiring new customers is much more expensive than retaining existing ones.

This project uses Machine Learning to identify customers with a high probability of churn so that the company can take proactive retention actions.

---

## Business Problem

Telecom companies lose thousands of customers every month.

Instead of contacting every customer manually, this system predicts which customers are most likely to leave the company.

Based on the prediction, the company can offer:

- Loyalty Rewards
- Discount Offers
- Better Customer Support
- Contract Upgrade
- Internet Security Trial

to retain valuable customers.
